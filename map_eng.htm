Biocesta is sited in the Malilla district. At Valencia South, between Ausias March Avenue and Malilla ctra. At North is the Trains Station and at South Turia river old channel.

There are two possible routes to go to Biocesta. Go to Olta street by Ausias March Avenue (Alicante direction) [A]. The other route is by the river, at the V-30 highway, way out Barrio Malilla, so you will be in Malilla ctra.. Then continue this road until the cross roads with Olta street.[B]
